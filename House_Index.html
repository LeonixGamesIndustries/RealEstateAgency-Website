This house is situated in the middle of Bel Air, near the Beverly Hills district of Los Angeles.
The house is a mansion and has two flats.
The first flat contains four of the seven bedrooms that are built in this house.
Every room has an own bathroom and sometimes has a balcony.
A pool is situated inside the ground floor and even an indoor and an outdoor dining room.
In the right side of the ground floor is situated a study room that is right in between the garages.
In the basement of the house ther is a whole cinema and in front of it you can see the bottom of the pool.
The house is meant for manteining the privacy of the people who lives inside it.
The mansion is situated a bit far away from the city center, but from there you should be able to see the famous Hollywood sign.
